Upcoming Facebook Live Masterclass

Diomine Digital Systems will soon host a professional **Facebook Live Masterclass** dedicated to **QGIS structural geology workflows**.

This technical training will focus on **plotting, stereographic analysis, and geological interpretation of structural data** using QGIS and stereonet tools. Participants will learn how to transform raw structural measurements into meaningful geological interpretations used in mineral exploration and structural mapping.

Topics include:

  • Structural data plotting in QGIS
  • Stereographic projection fundamentals
  • Interpretation of faults, folds and fracture systems
  • Structural geology analysis for exploration geology
  • Professional geological visualization techniques
