Structural mapping and stereographic analysis : Diomine DH Auditor 2026.1.2

This video showcases the advanced features of Diomine DH Auditor 2026, a tool designed to streamline the work of structural geologists and mining engineers through simplified stereographic analysis. Unlike other software that requires creating separate tables for each structure type, DH Auditor allows for the import of a global database (Excel or CSV) and uses dynamic filters to instantly isolate elements such as quartz veins or schistosity. The tutorial details visual plot customization, the choice between Schmidt projections (for statistics) and Wulff projections (for angles), as well as 3D data visualization. Finally, the author demonstrates how to export results, including an automatic calculation of Mean Plans (Main Plan), which is essential for precise future drill planning.

Auditing of field photographs and georeferenced texture inspection

In the competitive fields of geological exploration and mining, field data precision is the cornerstone of success, and the latest Diomine DH Auditor (v2026.1.2) introduces a powerful feature designed to bridge the gap between field photography and 3D modeling by transforming outcrop photos into actionable geospatial objects. By allowing geologists to precisely locate outcrops at their exact geographic position, attach direct geological descriptions to high-resolution images, and streamline audits through simplified data verification, this update ensures that field photographs are no longer static, disconnected files. The process, demonstrated by expert Diander Newton, is engineered for speed and intuition: users simply navigate to the "Import Georeferenced Image" tool, enter the precise X, Y, and Z coordinates, and enrich the data with technical metadata such as vein orientation or alteration zones. Beyond simple data management and grouping, the software enhances spatial interaction through an Interactive Pop-up mode for detailed texture inspection and Google Maps Integration for real-world topographic cross-referencing, ultimately revolutionizing the modern geologist's workflow and ensuring that with Diomine, "Mining becomes an easy game."

Optimization of Exploration and Grade control drillhole planning : Diomine DH Auditor 2026.1.2

Optimize Your Drill Planning with Diomine DH Auditor 2026.1.2: In this technical demonstration, expert Newton DIOMANDE showcases the power and flexibility of Diomine DH Auditor (v2026.1.2) for the precise planning of drilling programs and soil geochemistry. The software stands out with its unique import channel capable of handling various formats (CSV, DXF, GPX) and its seamless integration with Google Satellite basemaps for real-time geographic positioning. Thanks to the intuitive "Generate Drilling" tool, users can instantly configure point grids with automatic ID incrementing, define the orientation of survey lines (layons), and adjust drill spacing with millimeter precision. Management is further streamlined by a dynamic attribute table that allows for direct data export to Excel, GPX, or DXF, ensuring a fast and efficient workflow for both exploration and mine geologists.
