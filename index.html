Optimizing Drill Planning with Diomine DH Auditor 2026.1.2:
From Exploration to Grade Control

In the mining industry, the transition from exploration to production requires surgical precision. Diomine DH Auditor v2026.1.2 stands out as a versatile solution, capable of handling both wide exploration geochem grids and tight Grade Control (GC) patterns for open-pit or underground operations.

Unified Interface for Maximum Reactivity

Time is the most valuable resource on a mine site. DH Auditor allows importing structural data or block models via a single importation channel (CSV, DXF, GPX). Instant overlay on satellite imagery ensures drill locations are accessible and clear of existing mine infrastructure.

Grade Control: Planning for Precision

Precise Grade Control planning is vital to minimize dilution and maximize metal recovery. The "Generate Drilling" tool implements complex grids with ease:

  • ✔ 1. Intelligent Automation: Automatic ID sequencing (e.g., GC_PIT1_100, 101...) prevents fatal duplicates in production databases.
  • ✔ 2. Structural Alignment: Define your baseline and orient the grid according to the mineralized structure (e.g., Azimuth 030°) for optimal sampling.
  • ✔ 3. Real-time Flexibility: The "Refresh" function instantly re-sequences the attribute table if a point is moved due to field obstacles.

Interoperability: From Office to Pit

Export your plans instantly to industry standards: DXF/String for 3D design, Excel for drill sheets, and GPX/CSV for immediate field stake-out via GPS.
